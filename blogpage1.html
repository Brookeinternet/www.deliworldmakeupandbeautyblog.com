    Galactic Liner: Bold Graphic Eyeliner Looks

    Make your eyes the center of the universe with these bold, futuristic graphic liner looks! Perfect for parties, festivals, or creative nights out, these techniques use metallics, neons, and geometric shapes for serious impact.

    BASIC Steps

    Step 1: Prime & Prep

    Start with a clean eyelid and apply a matte primer or a light dusting of translucent powder for the sharpest lines and best color payoff.

    Step 2: Choose Your Liner

    Use a highly pigmented liquid liner (neon, metallic, white, or classic black), or try a gel or pencil for softer effects. For multicolored looks, pick a few shades that pop!

    Step 3: Graphic Shapes

    Try floating creases, double wings, negative space, or starbursts. For crisp edges, use tape or a stencil. Don’t be afraid to get creative—symmetry is cool, but asymmetry can be striking too!

    Step 4: Add Cosmic Accents

    Elevate your look with tiny dots, stars, or holographic gems along or above your liner. Pair with shimmery eyeshadow or leave the lids bare for extra drama.

    Step 5: Finishing Touches

    Add mascara and wispy lashes if desired. Keep lips and cheeks minimal to let your liner shine!

    BASIC Members Only Unlock with BASIC Subscribe to BASIC for access.
    VIP TIP Exclusive Steps for VIP Members

    Step 6: Space Dust Highlight

    Dab a holographic or iridescent highlighter on the inner corners of your eyes and just below the brow bone. This adds a multidimensional, cosmic effect that catches the light from every angle!

    Step 7: Interstellar Lower Lash Line

    For next-level drama, use a contrasting neon or metallic shade along your lower lash line. Smudge for a nebula-inspired haze or keep it sharp for a galactic pop.

    Step 8: Glitter Galaxy Freckles

    Using glitter gel or adhesive and cosmetic-safe glitter, tap a few "freckles" across your cheekbones and temples. This playful touch echoes stars scattered across the night sky!

    Step 9: Lock It In

    Mist your face with a dewy or illuminating setting spray to seal your look and add a subtle, spacey glow.

    Deli's Top 5 Must-Have Lipstick Brands (VIP Exclusive!)

    Lipstick is the ultimate accessory! A single swipe can transform your entire look and mood. After trying countless formulas and finishes, I've narrowed down my absolute top 5 lipstick brands that consistently deliver on color, comfort, and wear. Get ready to discover your new favorites!

    1. MAC Cosmetics: The Velvety Mattes

    MAC's matte lipsticks are legendary for a reason. Their Retro Matte and Powder Kiss formulas offer a sophisticated, non-drying matte finish with intense color payoff. From Ruby Woo to Velvet Teddy, their iconic shades are a staple in every makeup lover's collection.

    2. Charlotte Tilbury: The Hydrating Satins

    Charlotte Tilbury's K.I.S.S.I.N.G. and Pillow Talk lipsticks are pure luxury. These hydrating satin formulas glide on with a dream-like texture, providing a comfortable wear with a subtle sheen. They plump the lips and come in universally flattering shades.

    3. Maybelline SuperStay Matte Ink: Long-Wear Liquids

    For a liquid lipstick that truly lasts through meals and beyond, Maybelline's SuperStay Matte Ink is my top pick. These offer incredible pigment and a fierce matte finish that won't budge for up to 16 hours.

    4. Dior Addict Lip Glow: The Glossy Balms

    Dior's Lip Glow and Lip Glow Oil are the epitome of chic, comfortable lip color. These luxurious tinted balms and oils react to your lip's pH to create a custom shade, while providing intense hydration and a beautiful, non-sticky glossy finish.

    5. Bite Beauty Lip Lab: Custom Color Creations

    While the physical Bite Beauty Lip Labs might be limited, the concept of creating your own custom lipstick is still revolutionary. Imagine choosing your perfect shade, finish (matte, satin, glossy), and even flavor. It's an immersive experience that results in a truly personalized lipstick.

    Advanced Lipstick Tips (VIP Exclusive!)

    As a VIP member, you get access to my pro tips for flawless, long-lasting lipstick application. These techniques will elevate your lip game!

    • Prep is Key: Always exfoliate your lips gently and follow with a hydrating lip balm.
    • Conceal the Edges: For a super sharp look, lightly apply concealer around the perimeter of your lips.
    • Layering for Longevity: For maximum wear, apply a thin layer of lipstick, blot, and reapply.
    • Ombre Lips: For a fuller-looking pout, apply a darker liner or lipstick shade to the outer corners and fade inwards.
    • Fixing Feathering: If your lipstick tends to bleed or feather, try applying a clear liner or a bit of translucent powder around your lips.

    Deli's Top 5 Must-Have Blush & Eyeshadow Brands (VIP LOCKED)

    Elevate your entire makeup look with the perfect blush and eyeshadow! These two elements can transform your face, adding dimension, color, and depth. I've curated my top 5 favorite brands for both categories that consistently deliver stunning results for every occasion.

    Top 5 Must-Have Blush Brands

    1. Rare Beauty Soft Pinch Liquid Blush

    Rare Beauty's liquid blushes are an absolute phenomenon. They are incredibly pigmented, so a tiny dot goes a long way, and they blend out effortlessly to a natural, dewy finish. The shades are vibrant and flattering on a wide range of skin tones, leaving a beautiful, flushed look.

    2. NARS Blush (Orgasm)

    An iconic classic, NARS Orgasm blush lives up to its hype. This peachy-pink shade with a subtle golden shimmer is universally flattering and gives a gorgeous, natural flush. The finely-milled powder formula blends seamlessly and builds easily for more intensity.

    3. Hourglass Ambient Lighting Blush

    Hourglass blushes are a luxury experience. They combine the perfect amount of pigment with a diffused, luminous finish thanks to their Ambient Lighting Powder technology. They give a soft, airbrushed glow that looks incredibly natural and flattering, never chalky.

    4. Milani Baked Blush

    For an affordable yet high-performing option, Milani's Baked Blushes are incredible. They offer a beautiful radiant finish, making the cheeks look luminous and healthy. The pigmentation is excellent, and they blend effortlessly, often compared to high-end alternatives. Luminoso is a cult favorite for a reason!

    5. Glossier Cloud Paint

    If you love a sheer, buildable, and incredibly natural-looking flush, Glossier's Cloud Paint is your match. These gel-cream blushes are super easy to apply and blend, creating a diffused, watercolor-like effect on the cheeks. They give that coveted "lit-from-within" glow and are perfect for a minimalist makeup routine.

    Top 5 Must-Have Eyeshadow Brands

    1. Natasha Denona: The Pigment Queen

    Natasha Denona palettes are a splurge, but they are absolutely worth every penny for their unparalleled pigmentation and effortless blendability. Her eyeshadows feel like butter and truly pack a punch of color, whether you're working with mattes, shimmers, or metallics.

    2. Pat McGrath Labs: The Luxe Shimmers

    For truly mesmerizing metallics and glitters, Pat McGrath Labs is unmatched. Her Mothership palettes feature some of the most unique and dazzling special shades you'll ever encounter. The quality is supreme, and a single swipe delivers an intense, multi-dimensional sparkle that elevates any eye look.

    3. Anastasia Beverly Hills: The Blending Master

    Anastasia Beverly Hills palettes are known for their exceptional blendability and carefully curated color stories. Their matte shades are particularly easy to work with, allowing for seamless transitions and diffused edges. Palettes like Modern Renaissance are a classic for a reason.

    4. ColourPop Cosmetics: Affordable Quality

    ColourPop continually impresses with its innovative formulas and vast range of affordable eyeshadows. Their Super Shock Shadows are a unique bouncy cream-to-powder formula that applies beautifully with fingers, delivering intense shimmer. Their pressed powder palettes also offer great quality for the price.

    5. Urban Decay: The Long-Wear Legends

    Urban Decay palettes, especially the Naked series, revolutionized eyeshadow. They are celebrated for their smooth formulas, blendability, and impressive longevity. Their eyeshadows truly stay put all day without creasing, making them a reliable choice for any look.

    Master the art of color with these incredible blush and eyeshadow brands! Stay tuned for more premium beauty content coming soon to Deli's World!

    Daisy Freckles: Springtime Freckle Art

    Add whimsical daisy flowers to your freckles for a playful, festival-ready spring look.

    VIP Tutorial Locked →
    Step into spring with this adorable Daisy Freckles look! This playful trend adds tiny daisy flowers alongside natural or faux freckles, giving a whimsical, fresh-from-the-meadow effect. Perfect for festivals, parties, or anytime you want to celebrate your inner flower child.

    Step 1: Glowy Skin Base

    Begin with a dewy, luminous base. Use a hydrating primer and a light-to-medium coverage foundation or skin tint. Set only where needed to keep the skin looking fresh and glowy.

    Step 2: Faux Freckles

    Use a soft brown freckle pen, brow pen, or liquid eyeliner to dot freckles across the bridge of your nose and cheeks. Vary the size and placement for a natural effect. Tap gently with your fingertip to soften.

    Step 3: Painting Daisies

    Using a tiny detail brush and white face paint or liquid liner, draw small circles or five-petal flower shapes randomly over your freckle clusters. Add a yellow dot in the center of each daisy for a realistic finish. Space them out for a whimsical touch.

    Step 4: Soft Blush & Highlight

    Sweep a soft pink or peach blush across your cheeks and nose, blending over the freckles and daisies for that sun-kissed look. Top with a pearly highlighter for extra glow.

    Step 5: Finishing Details

    Complete the look with fluttery lashes, a swipe of pink lip gloss, and maybe a few tiny daisies at the outer corners of your eyes or temples for a festival-ready vibe!

    Enjoy this playful springtime look! Tag @delisworld with your daisy freckle creations for a chance to be featured.

    Bubblegum Pop: Y2K Pink Makeup (VIP Exclusive!)

    Channel your inner pop princess with this playful Y2K-inspired look! Bubblegum pinks, glossy lips, and shimmering eyes take center stage in this blast-from-the-past makeup tutorial.

    Step 1: Pink Base & Blush

    Go for a flawless, matte base using a soft-focus foundation. Apply a cool pink blush high on the apples of your cheeks and sweep it towards your temples for that signature Y2K flush.

    Step 2: Shimmery Pink Eyeshadow

    Use a frosty, pastel pink eyeshadow all over the lid, blending into the crease and along the lower lash line. For extra drama, pop a sparkly silver or white shimmer on the inner corners and brow bone.

    Step 3: Graphic Liner & Mascara

    Create a bold wing with white or silver liquid liner for that futuristic edge. Finish with several coats of lengthening mascara.

    Step 4: Glossy Bubblegum Lips

    Line your lips with a pink pencil and fill them in with a bright bubblegum pink lipstick. Top with a vinyl-shine gloss for that classic, juicy Y2K effect.

    Step 5: Rhinestone Accents (Optional!)

    For that true popstar vibe, add a few stick-on rhinestones under your eyes or at the outer corners.

    Peachy Glow: Soft Peach Makeup Tutorial (VIP Exclusive!)

    Embrace a warm, radiant look with this Peachy Glow tutorial! Soft peach tones flatter every skin tone and create a fresh, healthy vibe—perfect for any occasion, especially spring and summer.

    Step 1: Glowy Skin Prep

    Start with a hydrating primer and a luminous foundation or skin tint. Add a peachy liquid highlighter to the high points of your face for that lit-from-within glow.

    Step 2: Peach Blush Magic

    Choose a cream or powder blush in a soft peach shade. Smile and apply generously to the apples of your cheeks, blending upwards toward your temples. For extra warmth, dust a bit across your nose and chin.

    Step 3: Soft Peach Eyes

    Use a matte peach or coral eyeshadow all over your lids, blending softly into the crease. Add a touch of peach shimmer to the center of the lid and inner corners for dimension. Finish with brown mascara for a softer look.

    Step 4: Juicy Lips

    Line your lips with a nude-peach pencil and fill in with a glossy peach lip tint or balm. The goal: hydrated, plump, and natural.

    Celestial Glow: Sun, Moon & Stars Face Art (VIP Exclusive!)

    Shine like the night sky with this Celestial Glow face art tutorial! We'll combine golden sun rays, silvery moons, and sparkling star accents for a magical, festival-ready look that's sure to turn heads.

    Step 1: Luminous Base

    Start with a radiant foundation and lots of highlighter on the high points of your face—think cheekbones, nose bridge, and above your brows. This will create a glowing canvas for our celestial designs!

    Step 2: Painting the Sun & Moon

    Using gold and silver face paints or eyeliners, draw a small sun above one brow (gold rays radiating out) and a crescent moon on the opposite cheek or temple. Use a fine brush for precise lines. Highlight with shimmer or metallic eyeshadows for extra dimension.

    Step 3: Adding Stars

    Dot tiny stars using white liquid liner or face paint above your cheekbones, temples, and even along your brow bone. You can cluster a few together or scatter them like constellations.

    Step 4: Shimmery Eyes & Lips

    Apply a metallic eyeshadow in gold or silver to your lids, and use a matching shimmery highlighter on the inner corners and brow bone. For lips, choose a nude gloss or add a touch of gold/silver shimmer to the center for a cosmic effect.

    Your Celestial Glow is complete! Perfect for festivals, parties, or whenever you want to feel like a cosmic goddess. Share your looks with @delisworld!

    Ethereal Garden: Pastel Floral Face Paint (VIP Exclusive!)

    Become a blooming work of art with this Ethereal Garden look! Soft pastel petals, leafy accents, and delicate vines will transform your face into a dreamy spring garden. Perfect for festivals, creative shoots, or when you just want to feel extra magical.

    Step 1: Petal-Soft Base

    Begin with a glowy, even complexion. Use a dewy primer and a lightweight foundation. Skip heavy powder for a fresh, skin-like finish.

    Step 2: Mapping Out the Flowers

    With a white eyeliner or pastel pencil, lightly sketch where you want your flowers and vines to go—think along the cheekbones, temples, and even framing one eye.

    Step 3: Painting Pastel Petals

    Using pastel face paints or liquid eyeliners in pink, lavender, mint, yellow, and baby blue, fill in your sketched flowers. Add a bit of white or lighter color in the centers for dimension. Layer different petal shapes and sizes for a realistic, whimsical effect.

    Step 4: Vines & Leaves

    Use a fine brush and green or teal face paint to add delicate vines and leafy details connecting the flowers. Play with different shades for depth.

    Step 5: Glowy Highlights

    Add a pearly or iridescent highlighter to the tops of your cheekbones and on the petals for a dewy, magical glow.

    Step 6: Soft Eyes & Petal Lips

    Keep the eyes soft with pastel shadows and a hint of shimmer. Finish with a rosy pink or coral lip tint for a petal-fresh pout.

    Cosmic Chrome: Silver & Metallic Makeup Tutorial (VIP Exclusive!)

    Shine like a starship with this Cosmic Chrome tutorial! This metallic look is inspired by futuristic fashion and sci-fi movies, featuring silver shadows, reflective highlights, and a bold chrome lip.

    Step 1: Silver Base & Highlight

    Step 2: Metallic Eyes

    Step 5: Final Touches

    Add rhinestones at the outer corners of your eyes or across the brow for a true space-age vibe. Finish with volumizing mascara or a bold pair of false lashes.
