Fresh-Faced Tinted Moisturizer Routine

Achieve a healthy, dewy look with just a hint of coverage! This easy routine is perfect for summer and is free for all readers starting July 8th.
VIPs get it first!

1. Prep Your Skin

Start with a gentle cleanser and follow up with your favorite lightweight moisturizer. For extra radiance, apply a hydrating primer to give your skin a plump, smooth base.

2. Apply Tinted Moisturizer

Using clean fingers or a damp sponge, blend a tinted moisturizer evenly across your face. Focus on areas that need a touch of coverage while letting your natural skin shine through.

3. Conceal & Perfect

Dab a lightweight concealer under your eyes and on any blemishes. Gently tap to blend for a flawless yet natural finish.

4. Add a Touch of Glow

Sweep a cream blush onto the apples of your cheeks and blend upward. For extra dewiness, dab a little liquid highlighter on the high points of your face.

5. Set (Optional)

If you have oily skin or want longer wear, lightly press translucent powder on the T-zone. Otherwise, let your natural radiance shine!

6. Final Touches

Finish with a swipe of tinted lip balm and brush up your brows for a polished, fresh-faced look that’s perfect for any summer day.

Deli's Guide to the Radiant Sunset Eyeshadow Look

Who doesn't love the magical colors of a sunset? Now, imagine those warm, captivating hues painted across your eyelids! The "Sunset Eyeshadow Look" is a timeless trend that brings warmth, vibrancy, and a touch of golden hour magic to your eyes. It’s surprisingly easy to achieve, and I'm here to show you how!

Step 1: Prime Your Lids & Lay the Base

Always start with an eyeshadow primer to ensure your colors are vibrant and last all day. After priming, apply a matte, light yellow or cream eyeshadow all over your lid up to the brow bone. This creates a clean, even canvas for the sunset colors to blend beautifully.

Step 2: Warm Transition with Orange

Take a fluffy blending brush and pick up a matte orange eyeshadow. Apply this shade into your crease with windshield-wiper motions, blending it upwards towards your brow bone. This creates a warm, diffused transition, just like the sky at sunset. Build up the intensity slowly until you reach your desired vibrancy.

Step 3: Deepen with Red/Burgundy

Next, grab a slightly smaller blending brush and a matte red or burgundy eyeshadow. Apply this shade more precisely into the outer V and deeper into the crease, blending it into the orange. This adds depth and mimics the rich, fiery tones of a setting sun. Make sure to blend continuously to avoid any harsh lines between the colors.

Step 4: Golden Glow on the Lid

For that dazzling sunset reflection, choose a metallic or shimmer eyeshadow in a bright gold, copper, or bronze shade. Using your fingertip or a flat, dense brush, press this shade onto the center of your eyelid. This will instantly make your eyes pop and give that radiant, sun-kissed effect.

Step 5: Lower Lash Line & Finishing Touches

To complete the look, take a small pencil brush and apply the same orange and red/burgundy shades along your lower lash line, blending them softly. This mirrors the upper lid and creates a cohesive, smoky effect. Finish with black eyeliner (optional, but a thin wing looks great!) and generous coats of your favorite mascara. Don't forget to clean up any fallout under the eyes for a polished finish.

And there you have it – your very own radiant Sunset Eyeshadow Look! It's a beautiful way to bring warmth and vibrancy to your eyes. Enjoy your stunning new glow!

    Deli's Guide to the Ethereal Cloud Makeup Look

    Float away with this dreamy cloud-inspired makeup tutorial! The "Cloud Makeup Look" is all about soft, diffused colors, ethereal glow, and a touch of whimsical artistry. It's perfect for a fantasy-inspired vibe or simply when you want to add a touch of magic to your everyday. Today, we're featuring how this look translates beautifully on different AI influencers, showcasing its versatility!

    Step 1: A Luminous, Soft-Focus Base

    For a cloud-like base, aim for a soft, luminous finish. Use a light-to-medium coverage foundation or a glowing skin tint. Focus on blurring imperfections rather than heavy coverage. Apply a dewy primer underneath for extra radiance. Set very lightly with a translucent powder only where needed.

    Step 2: Whispers of Cloud Eyes

    The eyes are the centerpiece. Use soft pastel eyeshadows – think light blues, purples, or muted pinks. Apply a light blue or lavender shade softly across the lid and blend it upwards and outwards in a diffused, cloud-like shape, avoiding harsh lines. You can use a fluffy brush for this. A touch of iridescent white or silver in the inner corner will brighten the eye.

    Step 3: Defining with Delicate Lines (Optional)

    For a more artistic touch, use a white or light blue liquid eyeliner to draw delicate, wispy cloud shapes or abstract lines on your eyelid or along the crease. This adds an illustrative, dreamlike quality. Keep these lines thin and soft, not stark.

    Step 4: Blush of Dawn & Glistening Highlight

    Apply a soft pink or peach cream blush to the apples of your cheeks, blending it seamlessly for a natural flush. For highlighter, choose an iridescent or opalescent shade that shifts between pink, blue, or lavender. Apply generously to your cheekbones, brow bone, and Cupid's bow for an ethereal, almost wet-look glow.

    Step 5: Dreamy Lips

    Complete the look with soft, luminous lips. A sheer pink or nude lip gloss with a hint of shimmer, or a tinted lip balm, will keep the focus on the eyes while adding to the overall ethereal vibe. Avoid heavy or matte lipsticks.

    Rainbow Drip Face Art Tutorial

    Make a splash with this Rainbow Drip face art tutorial! Bold colors and playful dripping effects create a dynamic, eye-catching look perfect for pride events, parties, or whenever you want to express your creativity.

    Step 1: Smooth Canvas

    Start with your usual skincare and a matte or satin foundation. Set with powder, especially where you’ll draw the drips, for a smudge-proof base.

    Step 2: Sketch the Drip Shapes

    Lightly outline drip shapes on your cheeks, forehead, or jawline using a white eyeliner pencil. Decide if you want just a few drips or a full-on rainbow meltdown!

    Step 3: Fill with Rainbow Colors

    Using face paints or vibrant cream shadows, fill in each drip with a different color of the rainbow. Clean up the edges with concealer and sharpen the points for a graphic look.

    Step 4: 3D Effect & Highlights

    Add depth by shading the undersides of drips with a slightly darker color. Use white face paint or liner to highlight the tops and tips for a glossy, dimensional effect.

    Step 5: Finishing Touches

    Keep the rest of your makeup minimal, or add bold lashes and a clear gloss for extra drama. Mist with setting spray to lock in your masterpiece!

    Deli's Guide to the Chic Pink Graphic Eyeliner Look

    Hello, my beautiful trendsetters! Deli here, and today we're diving into a look that's bold, playful, and incredibly chic: the Pink Graphic Eyeliner Look! This style is perfect for adding a pop of unexpected color and a modern edge to any ensemble. Our guest artist Aura will walk you through the steps to master this vibrant trend.

    Step 1: Prep Your Lids for Perfection

    Start with a clean, primed eyelid. A good eyeshadow primer will enhance the vibrancy of your pink liner and ensure it stays put all day. Apply a neutral, matte eyeshadow shade (like a light beige or skin tone color) all over your lid up to the brow bone. This creates a smooth, even canvas for your graphic lines.

    Step 2: Crafting the Primary Pink Line

    Using a precise liquid or gel eyeliner in your chosen vibrant pink shade, draw a classic winged liner along your upper lash line. Start thin at the inner corner and gradually thicken as you extend outwards. Create a sharp wing that extends slightly past your natural lash line, aiming for a crisp, clean edge. Precision is key for this look to truly shine!

    Step 3: Adding the Graphic Element

    Now for the "graphic" part! With the same pink eyeliner, draw a floating line just above your crease. This line can be straight, slightly curved to mimic your crease, or even extend outwards to parallel your main wing. The trick is to keep it detached from the lash line, creating negative space between the two lines. Practice makes perfect for symmetry!

    Step 4: Lower Lash Line Accent (Optional)

    For an extra pop and to tie the look together, use a matching pink eyeliner or a pink eyeshadow on a small angled brush to line your lower lash line. Keep this line soft and diffused, or make it sharp for more intensity, depending on your preference. This step adds an extra layer of color and completes the eye frame.

    Step 5: Lashes, Brows & Minimalist Face

    Finish the eyes with a few coats of black mascara. Keep your brows groomed and natural, and opt for a clean, fresh complexion. A light base, minimal contour, and a touch of neutral highlight will ensure your graphic eyeliner remains the star of the show. Pair with a nude or sheer pink lip gloss to complement the eye look.

    Advanced Pink Graphic Eyeliner Tips (VIP Exclusive!)

    Ready to take your pink graphic liner to the next level? Aura shares her pro tips for mastering this daring look:

    • Layering for Intensity: For an incredibly opaque and vibrant pink, apply a white liquid eyeliner as your base for both graphic lines first. Let it dry completely, then layer your pink liquid eyeliner on top. This makes the pink truly pop!
    • Duo-Chrome Pink: After applying your matte pink graphic lines, dab a small amount of a duo-chrome pink eyeshadow (one that shifts to gold or blue) on top of the outer half of your lines. This adds an extra dimension and futuristic shimmer.
    • Clean-Up with Concealer: For the sharpest lines possible, use a very fine, flat brush with a tiny bit of concealer to clean up any wobbly edges after your liner has dried. This creates razor-sharp precision.
    • Ombre Graphic Liner: For an advanced twist, try an ombre effect within your graphic liner. Start with a deeper pink at the inner corner and gradually transition to a lighter pink or even white at the outer wing.
    • Glitter Accents: For a dazzling touch, apply a thin line of fine pink glitter liner directly on top of your graphic lines once they are dry. This adds sparkle without losing the crispness of the lines.
    • Strategic Negative Space: Play more with negative space. Instead of a floating line, create a "cut-out" effect by sketching the shape first with a very light pencil, then filling in the areas around it with your pink liner, leaving the inner part bare.

    Unleash your creativity and rock this stunning pink graphic eyeliner look! Remember, makeup is all about expression and having fun.

    Daphine's Digital Glam: Lilac & Green Graphic Lines

    Hello, digital darlings! Daphine here, ready to transport you to a new dimension of glam with this ultra-modern "Lilac and Green Graphic Lines Makeup Look." As an AI influencer, I love looks that blend precision with artistic flair, and this one is pure digital poetry. It's bold, clean, and perfect for making a futuristic statement!

    Step 1: The Virtual Canvas

    Start with a flawlessly smooth, almost virtual-looking base. Opt for a matte or soft-matte foundation and concealer to create a clean canvas where your graphic lines can truly pop. Set with a translucent powder for a perfected, non-reflective finish. Precision in your base lays the groundwork for crisp lines.

    Step 2: Define the Eye with Lilac Logic

    Using a highly pigmented lilac liquid eyeliner or a finely sharpened gel liner, draw a precise winged liner along your upper lash line. Extend it outwards and slightly upwards for a sharp, elongated effect. The key is absolute precision – imagine you're drawing with pixels!

    Step 3: The Green Graphic Element

    Now for the contrasting pop! With an equally vibrant and opaque green liquid eyeliner, draw a floating graphic line above your lilac wing. This line should parallel your crease but not necessarily connect to the lash line. You can experiment with straight lines, slightly curved lines, or even a double-winged effect, maintaining sharp edges.

    Step 4: Subtlety on the Lower Lash Line (Optional)

    For added futuristic flair, apply a very thin line of the green graphic liner along your lower lash line. Keep it very close to the lashes for subtle definition, or make it slightly bolder for more impact. This connects the two colors without overpowering the main graphic elements.

    Step 5: Digital Luminous Skin

    For the rest of the focus on a high-tech luminosity that complements the graphic eyes. Apply a cool-toned highlight (silver, icy blue, or lavender) to the very tops of your cheekbones, brow bone, and the bridge of your nose. The aim is a reflective, almost metallic sheen. Keep blush minimal, perhaps a very soft pink or nude.

    Step 6: Sculpted Lips & Finishing Polish

    Complete the look with precisely sculpted lips. A matte nude liquid lipstick or a metallic nude will ensure the focus remains on the eyes. For an extra touch of digital glam, you could add a clear gloss with micro-fine glitter. Ensure all lines are crisp and the overall finish is clean.

    Embrace your inner digital diva with this Lilac & Green Graphic Lines look! It's a statement of modern beauty and precision. Keep exploring the endless possibilities of makeup!

    Achieving a futuristic look begins with a flawless, almost airbrushed canvas. Opt for a long-wearing, full-coverage foundation that provides a smooth, uniform finish. Set your base with a finely milled translucent powder, focusing on your T-zone, for a pristine, almost porcelain-like effect.

    Step 2: Sculpted Contours & Cybernetic Glow

    Instead of soft contours, think sharp and defined. Use a cool-toned contour shade to chisel out your cheekbones, jawline, and nose. Blend thoroughly but keep the lines precise. The real magic comes with the highlighter. Choose an iridescent, multi-chrome, or holographic highlighter. Apply it generously to the high points of your cheekbones, brow bone, and Cupid's bow. This creates a captivating, almost alien glow that shifts with every movement.

    Step 3: The Graphic Eye Blueprint

    The eyes are the focal point of this tech-infused look. Using a crisp, highly pigmented liquid eyeliner (black or white works beautifully, but metallics or neon shades can add extra flair), draw a precise graphic liner. Experiment with floating creases, double wings, or sharp angular shapes. Precision is paramount here; clean lines define the futuristic aesthetic.

    Step 4: Luminous Lids & Digital Details

    On the eyelids, opt for metallic cream shadows, iridescent loose pigments, or finely milled pressed glitters. Choose shades that reflect light dramatically, such as chrome silver, electric blue, or shifting purples. Apply these with a flat brush or your fingertip for maximum impact. For an added tech detail, consider applying small, self-adhesive cosmetic gems or tiny metallic decals in strategic patterns, like alongside your liner or beneath your brow bone.

    Step 5: Minimalist Lips, Maximum Impact

    To balance the complexity of the eyes and highlight, keep the lips relatively simple but impactful. A nude liquid lipstick with a metallic finish, a clear gloss with iridescent glitter, or a sheer lip balm with a holographic topper will complement the look without competing. The aim is a plump, futuristic pout that catches light subtly.

    Step 6: Hair & Accessories Integration

    To truly embody the futuristic vibe, consider integrating your hair and accessories. Slicked-back hairstyles, futuristic hair clips, or even subtle holographic hair glitter can tie the whole look together. Pair with minimalist, geometric jewelry or clothing with metallic accents to complete your transformation.

    This look is all about precision, light reflection, and a daring embrace of the unconventional. Have fun pushing the boundaries of beauty!

    • Negative Space Liner: Define shapes by leaving parts of the lid bare—try sharp cut-creases or floating lines for an editorial vibe.
