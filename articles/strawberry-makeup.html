Deli's Guide to the Sweet Strawberry Makeup Look

Get ready to look as sweet and fresh as a strawberry! This trending makeup look is all about achieving a youthful, sun-kissed, and subtly flushed complexion, reminiscent of ripe strawberries. It's natural, vibrant, and perfect for spring and summer. Let's dive into how to achieve this adorable style.

Step 1: Fresh, Dewy Skin Base

The foundation of this look is glowing, healthy skin. Opt for a lightweight tinted moisturizer, BB cream, or a dewy-finish foundation. Focus on evening out your skin tone rather than heavy coverage. Conceal only where absolutely necessary. Finish with a hydrating setting spray to lock in the dewiness.

Step 2: The Signature Strawberry Blush

This is the star of the show! Choose a vibrant liquid or cream blush in a berry, strawberry-red, or bright pink shade. Apply it generously across the apples of your cheeks, extending slightly upwards towards your temples, and lightly across the bridge of your nose. This mimics a natural, sun-kissed flush. Blend well for a seamless, diffused effect.

Step 3: Faux Freckles (Optional, but Adorable!)

To enhance the natural, youthful charm of this look, consider adding faux freckles. Use a freckle pen, a brow pen, or even a soft brown eyeliner pencil. Dot small, irregular marks across your nose and cheeks where the blush is most concentrated. Gently tap with your finger to blend and soften them for a more natural appearance.

Step 4: Sweet & Simple Eyes

Keep the eyes minimal and fresh to let the blush shine. A sheer wash of a soft pink or peachy eyeshadow across the lids is perfect. A subtle winged liner using a brown or black liquid liner can define the eyes, and a few coats of mascara will open them up.

Step 5: Juicy, Glossy Lips

Complete the look with juicy, hydrated lips. Opt for a tinted lip balm or a sheer lip gloss in a cherry red, berry, or pink shade. The goal is a plump, succulent finish that complements the flushed cheeks.
